A lifelong passion I have is music. I started studying piano as a kid, then growing up I experimented with different instruments: classical guitar, electric bass and drums. Only when I finally discovered and explored a little bit Johann Sebastian Bach's music I decided to dedicate myself to a deeper study of keyboard instruments.

For five years I attended Istituto Civico Musicale "L. Rocca" in Alba where, under the teaching of M° Omar Caputi, I studied piano and pipe organ. Simultaneously I developed a taste for other keyboard instruments such as synthesizers, harpsichord and keyboards.

I played, amongst others, with Loschi Dezi and Luca Morino's solo project (synth, piano and organ), with pop punk band Dance! No Thanks(drums) and am currently playing and arranging in Follia Nuda.
